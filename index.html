Who I Am

I am a PhD candidate in AI and Machine Learning at New Jersey Institute of Technology, with a 4.0 GPA and a research focus on intelligent systems that bridge the gap between cutting-edge ML and real-world deployment.

My work spans probabilistic deep learning, agentic AI systems, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines. I have built and deployed end-to-end ML workflows on datasets exceeding 700,000 observations, achieving state-of-the-art results (R² = 0.986) through rigorous feature engineering and Bayesian hyperparameter optimization.

As the Entrepreneurial Lead of the NSF I-Corps SmartComfort startup (awarded $53,000), I combine deep technical expertise with product thinking — translating research into systems that solve real problems at scale.
